Introduction

Our goal is to use advances in technology to enhance the teaching and learning of molecular biology.

In particular, we strive to increase the understanding of molecular biology through interactive multi-media materials.

Our website incorporates a variety of interactions with animations and tutorials where appropriate. These materials are designed to augment rather than replace textbooks and lectures.
